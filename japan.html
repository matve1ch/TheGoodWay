HOW TO GO

From UAE to Japan can be reached by direct flights of Emirates and Etihad Airways, which fly to Tokyo and Osaka . The journey time is approximately 9-10 hours. Connected flights via Doha, Istanbul or other Asian hubs are also available. After arriving in Japan, you can take Shinkansen high-speed trains or domestic flights to travel between cities.

WHEN TO GO

The best time to travel to Japan depends on your preferences. Spring is the cherry blossom season, especially in Tokyo, Kyoto and Osaka, when parks and streets are transformed into picturesque pink landscapes. Autumn is famous for the vibrant colours of the foliage and comfortable weather. Summer is hot and humid, but colourful festivals such as Gion-matsuri and Tanabata take place. Winter is ideal for skiing in Hokkaido and attending the famous snow festivals.

THINGS TO DO

Arashiyama Bamboo Forest is located in Kyoto and is one of the most picturesque parts of the country. The tall green stalks create a mysterious atmosphere, and the slight sound of the wind in the bamboo thickets is soothing and mesmerising. Shibazakura Park is a paradise for nature lovers. In spring, thousands of shibazakura flowers bloom here, colouring the ground with pink, white and purple hues. Fushimi Inari-taisha is one of Kyoto's most famous Shinto temples, known for its thousands of bright red torii gates. They form picturesque tunnels leading to the top of Mount Inari. Aogashima is one of Japan's most remote and mysterious islands. Located in the Philippine Sea, this volcanic island is surrounded by steep cliffs and dense forests.
