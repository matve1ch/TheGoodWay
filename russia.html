HOW TO GO

You can get from the UAE to Russia by direct flights operated by Emirates, FlyDubai, Aeroflot, Etihad Airways and other airlines. The flight time from Dubai or Abu Dhabi to Moscow is about 5-6 hours. There are also direct flights to St. Petersburg, Kazan, Ekaterinburg and other major cities. Travelling to remote regions, such as Kamchatka or the Caucasus, will require an additional flight or train journey.

WHEN TO GO

The best time to travel to Russia depends on the purpose of your trip. In summer, the climate is most comfortable for sightseeing and city walks. In spring and autumn, nature is especially beautiful and there are fewer tourists. In winter it is worth coming if you want to see snow-covered Russia, ski in the Caucasus mountains or enjoy the icy beauty of Lake Baikal.

THINGS TO DO

Lake Baikal is one of the deepest and cleanest lakes in the world. In winter its surface turns into transparent ice, creating fantastic landscapes, while in summer you can explore wild beaches, crystal clear water and rich nature. Mount Elbrus is the highest peak in Russia and Europe, located in the Caucasus. It is a popular destination for mountaineers and skiers. Krenitsyn Volcano is one of the most beautiful and hard-to-reach places in Kamchatka. It is located on Onekotan Island and is a volcano inside a crater lake. Peterhof is a luxurious palace and park complex near St Petersburg, built on the orders of Peter the Great. Here you can see famous fountains, magnificent palaces and manicured gardens that create the atmosphere of the Tsarist era.
