HOW TO GO

From the UAE to Italy can be reached by direct flights with Emirates, Etihad Airways and FlyDubai. The flight time from Dubai or Abu Dhabi to Rome, Milan or Venice is approximately 6-7 hours. Connected flights via European hubs such as Frankfurt, Paris or Istanbul are also available. Within Italy, it is most convenient to travel by high-speed trains, hire car or domestic flights for long-haul routes.

WHEN TO GO

The best time to travel to Italy depends on your preferences. Spring and autumn are ideal seasons for a comfortable holiday: pleasant weather, fewer tourists and a rich cultural life. Summer is suitable for beach holidays on the coast, but it can be hot in the cities. In winter, Italy is worth visiting for the Christmas atmosphere, ski resorts in the Alps and a quiet holiday without crowds of tourists.

THINGS TO DO

The island of Capri is located in the Gulf of Naples and is known for its rocky shores, azure waters and the Blue Grotto, a unique cave where the water glows an incredible shade of blue. The Dolomite Alps are one of the most beautiful places in Italy, popular with outdoor enthusiasts. In summer, it offers trekking along scenic routes, while in winter resorts such as Cortina d'Ampezzo become ideal skiing destinations. The Val d'Orcia Valley is a picturesque region in Tuscany, famous for its undulating hills, cypress alleys and vineyards. It is the perfect place to sample Tuscan wines, discover rural Italy and enjoy authentic scenery. Villa d'Este is a luxurious Renaissance villa located in Tivoli, near Rome. It is known for its magnificent gardens and cascade of fountains, which are considered a masterpiece of landscape art.
